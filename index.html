ALL ABOUT ME

That's why we create personal websites

I landed into Computer science by accident, in my grad. But ever since I took my first class on C language, things have changed. I was so moved with the idea that by learning a language to talk to computers, you can make a computer do whatever you want. It was magical! It was like Big Bang and start of time.

I choose to pursue Masters in Computer Applications rather than in my previous obsession Electronics. Thought I had to leave Electronics behind, what I have learned thus far has helped understand Behind the curtain things of a Computer. The 6 years of classroom study has tough me languages like C, C++, Java, SQL, HTML, JavaScript, Shell scripting, Assembly and key software development concepts like Data bases and Operating Systems. To give a graceful exit I ended up writing boot loader and a tiny operating system for my final year project. I have stepped out of college with 3 offers in hand.

I started my career as an MS SQL developer and touched VBA and SQL server integration services on the way. I was good at what I was doing but didn't enjoy as much as developing websites with Java, Servlets, JSP and struts. I ended up switching the technology when I got an opportunity to work with Adobe Experience Manager (AEM), an enterprise content management system from Adobe. It used to be called CQ5 back then.

It's been 8 years with the technology now and I loved developing enterprise websites for Retail grocery, Telecommunications and Health care domains. I was also able to transition from dealing with compiler errors to finding application problems in massive infrastructure. I currently enjoy working as an AEM Architect for a Health care client. I never took break from coding, may be I never will. I come home and work on something , like programming micro controllers like Arduino and tinkering with single board computers like Raspberry Pi.

No, I am not a Nerd. I travel a lot, kayak whenever I get a chance and may hop onto a Jet ski, watch Japanese Anime, create video content, make exotic food, mix drinks, accumulate unwanted gadgets and dance like no one is watching.
